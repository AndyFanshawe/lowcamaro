Cobra kit car build

Back in about 1990 I was approached by a chinese policeman in the East end of London about the possibility of building him a cobra (Pilgrim Sumo) kit car.

I agreed to do this and it took about 4 years from start to finish!

Luckily I met my Wife to be in 1993 and she persuaded me to complete it quicker!!

It was built in 2 council garages in Halfway in Sheffield with no power!!  Any work that needed to be done had to be taken to my home and worked on there,
