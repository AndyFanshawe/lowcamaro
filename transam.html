1977 Pontiac Trans Am 6.6 litre NOS

  • This was my very first american car project.  Bought from Shuttlewood in Bolsover in the summer of 1987 as a good looker, but rusty relic, but driveable.
  • It was stripped, media blasted and metalwork repaired.  Then resprayed bright red.
  • Over the space of the next few years till its sale in may 1994, it was turned into a 1000 hp nitrous injected monster!

 

  • It was used on the dragstrip in York in 1992 and 1993.

  • This is one of the last photos of it on the 24th May 1994 at Doncaster Exhibition Centre with my lovely girlfriend, Carol (now my Wife!).

  • 6 weeks later, we bought the 86 Camaro which also appears on the home page
