Welcome to Andy's American Kustoms!

 

Thank you for visiting my website.

This is my 1 man business to restore, repair or modify just about anything!

Doesn't even have to be cars!!

This started at age of 16 when my Dad got me a very rusty 1970 Hillman Avenger back in 1980! 

Needless to say it didn't stay standard for long.

Interestingly, I have been a full time 11- 18 Maths teacher in Derbyshire for the last 34 years and cars have been an obsessive hobby in my own time.  Now its time for a career change!

I may be in my 50's but I still design, build and drive highly modified cars!

A mature boy racer.

I have a degree in Maths, and another in digital electronics, but am qualified in most things!!

Diagnostics using oscilloscopes, scan tools and test lights are skills that I continue to work on everyday.

But my main passion is bringing old machinery back to life and perhaps injecting some new technology at the same time!!
